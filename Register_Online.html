Stages of Rally

A vintage rally typically include three stages, each of which has its own beginning and end. However, not all stages are created equally. One stage is more important than others.

1.Super Stage
This is probably what's imagine when someone says "rally". It take place on a empty road close to public and ranges from a kilometer to several tens of kilometres. Every vintage car will make a round trip. 2.Power Stage
In every VRCSA rally, at the end of the event is known as "power stage". It is broadcast on TV and social meida. There are extra points for the three crews whose car still have original components. 3.Transport Stage
The final stage is known as transport stage. The vintage car will be driven in normal traffic while obeying national road law to showoff the beauty. The winner will be selected after end of final stage.

Countries Overview: Annual events are held in one of these countries that are part of South East Asia. Those countries are Myanmar, Laos, Vietnam, Singapore and Thailand. Southeast Asia has these things in spades. It's also steeped in rich, ancient customs and traditions that are remarkably different from those of the West.
